About the company

One truck.
Now a market leader.

Founded in 1987 by Mr. Rashid Jhungeer, JSTL® grew from a single truck operation into one of the leading heavy-duty logistics and lifting companies in Mauritius and Madagascar.

Today, under the next generation of family leadership, we deliver safe, efficient projects with state-of-the-art equipment, a highly skilled workforce and a culture of accountability — taking responsibility for our actions, delivering on our promises, and holding the highest standards of integrity on every site.

Committed to professionalism in delivering safe and efficient projects. At JSTL® we ensure safe and efficient project delivery through professional solutions, state-of-the-art equipment and expert techniques — exceeding client expectations with a commitment to excellence and meticulous attention to detail.
